OKR methodology

We will be leading the Delivery process and will be using OKR methodology (Google & Intel's project management methodology for innavation teams). It is highly agile and effective, however It is totally different than the Scrum based Agile practices. In our experiences, this is far more faster, reliable and creative for small teams.

Agile Project





Who is this for ?

You have an idea and many other sub-ideas
You have a decision board
You have time and money resources
Who should avoid this ?

Startups on lower budget
Your decision maker is a comitee (of employees and vendors, customers,etc.)

What is a Typical Agile PM consist of ?


  • Daily scrums
  • Weekly Refinement
  • Sprint reviews (usually 2 weeks)
  • Sprint start
There is a huge trend in the market to deliver projects with Agile delivery. There are some certain projects and cases, this methodoligies are super beneficial. It is specifically very good for big coorparates to monitor their numbers with big metrics.

We usually don't recommend classical Agile Project Management approach to Startup companies. As Startups need a competetive edge against the market, and surely this is one of the item. A small startup with high agility will have and advantage of this heavy weight sumo fighter 'Agile PM practices'
